About Revisidus Earth

Revisidus Earth LLC is a mission-driven small business. Our core purpose is to remove metal waste from the environment, turn it into funds for charitable causes, and celebrate the beauty of the planet through handmade art.

  • Responsible scrap collection & sorting
  • Fair, transparent resale to recycling yards
  • Donations to vetted non-profits
  • Original art crafted from earth-and-metal motifs

After each recycling sale, we donate the proceeds to a selected charity. Quarterly, we release a brief impact note detailing weight recycled, sale amount, and donation receipts.

Want to suggest a charity? Reach out‚Äîwe'd love to collaborate.

Our art line channels textures of ore, patina, and stone into wall pieces, small sculptures, and prints. Profits from art support our business and enable us to scale the donation effort.

  • Limited-edition wall art (metal + mixed media)
  • Small desk sculptures
  • Fine-art prints available online

Recycling metal significantly reduces energy use and emissions compared to mining virgin ore. Every pound diverted from landfill is a win for the planet.

  • Lower greenhouse gas emissions vs. raw extraction
  • Conserves natural resources & reduces pollution
  • Encourages circular economy in local communities
